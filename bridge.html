Bridge is one of my favorite activities in leisure times. I am an active member of American Contract Bridge League (ACBL). My current ACBL rank is Sectional Master. ACBL is the second largest of eight zonal organizations of World Bridge Federation (WBF). I play bridge in Lafayette Duplicate Bridge Club, an ACBL sanctioned bridge club.
