 Paper Registration
  • Deadline: June 21, 2010.
  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register at the full rate of US$500. Authors with multiple accepted papers must pay an additional fee of US$100 per paper for the second and subsequent papers. Payment must be received by June 21, 2010 in order for the paper to be included in the proceedings.
  • Registration rate applies to both main conference and workshops.
Early Registration
  • Deadline: July 15, 2010.
  • Regular rate: US$500. This will cover the cost of one copy of the proceedings, all sessions, lunches, coffee break, and banquet.
  • Student rate: US$100. This will cover the cost of one copy of the proceedings, all sessions, lunches, coffee break, but no banquet. Each banquet ticket can be purchased at 45$.
  • Registration rate applies to both main conference and workshops.
Late/On-site Registration
  • Regular rate: US$600. This will cover the cost of one copy of the proceedings, all sessions, lunches, coffee break, and banquet.
  • Student rate: US$150. This will cover the cost of one copy of the proceedings, all sessions, lunches, coffee break, but no banquet. Each banquet ticket can be purchased at 45$.
  • Registration rate applies to both main conference and workshops.
