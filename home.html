I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Texas A&M University - Kingsville. Network Dynamics and Simulation Science Laboratory in Biocomplexity Institute of Virginia Tech, Blacksburg, Virginia.--> I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Purdue University, M.S. in Computer Science from North Dakota State University and B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology.

My research interests are big data analytics, high performance computing, network science, data mining, parallel and distributed algorithms, randomized algorithms, and wireless ad-hoc networks.

Besides Computer Science, I have interests in Astronomy and Space Science. I love to play contract bridge whenever I get a chance.
