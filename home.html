I am working as a Research Scientist at Network Dynamics and Simulation Science Laboratory at Biocomplexity Institute, Virginia Tech, Blacksburg, Virginia. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Purdue University, M.S. in Computer Science from North Dakota State University and B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology.

My research interests are big data analytics, high performance computing, network science, data mining, parallel and distributed algorithms, randomized algorithms, and wireless ad-hoc networks.

Besides Computer Science, I have interests on Astronomy and Space Science. Contract bridge and planting trees are my passions.
