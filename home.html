I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Texas A&M University - Kingsville. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Purdue University, M.S. in Computer Science from North Dakota State University and B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology. 

My research interests are big data, graph analytics, high performance computing, parallel and distributed algorithms, randomized algorithms, and data mining. 

Besides Computer Science, I have interests in Astronomy and Space Science. Traveling and playing contract bridge are my favorite hobbies.
